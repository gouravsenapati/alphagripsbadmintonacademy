Training Venues

Train Across Two Competitive Badminton Venues In Bengaluru

AlphaGrips sessions are delivered across Shuttle Smash Sports Studio in Panduranga Nagar and DNA Badminton Arena in Akshayanagar, giving players a consistent indoor training and match-play environment across South Bengaluru.

Our Venue Network

We train out of two badminton-focused indoor venues so athletes can access structured coaching, regular sparring, and match-style practice closer to where they live.
