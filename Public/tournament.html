How viewers use it

Open the tournament and choose your event

Use the tournament viewer to switch events, inspect draws, watch court assignments, check live match status, and open the official draw sheet.

How staff use it

Login only when you need tournament control

Tournament staff should use the dedicated tournament console after login. That keeps regular AlphaGrips admin workflows separate from live tournament operations.

How referees and coaches use it

Login and take an open court

Court officials can log in to the court console, select an open court, then start, score, and complete only the matches assigned to that court.
